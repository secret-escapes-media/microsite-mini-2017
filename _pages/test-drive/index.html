Off the beaten track

No matter which road you choose, the new MINI Countryman accommodates in comfort.

Whether you’re munching miles on the motorway, or taking the dirt track into the unknown, the new MINI Countryman’s ALL4 design steers you clear of even the toughest situations. All-wheel drive is dynamically applied by the system to ensure the best handling on the trickiest of terrains. When you reach your off-the-beaten-path destination, pull up and enjoy the view from the new MINI Countryman’s optional picnic bench.

Add space

There’s nothing cramped about the new MINI Countryman – it comfortably seats five adults, with plenty legroom to ensure everyone travels in comfort.

Alternatively, pile up the luggage, with enough space to ensure you’ll have far too much stuff for any country escape. The large boot can accommodate any itinerary, with 13cm of travel from the rear seats ensuring no space is left unused.

Safety first

For the most intrepid of explorers, the new MINI Countryman is designed to keep you and your loved ones safe.

The electric parking brake makes hill starts hassle free, particularly useful when tackling steep mountain passes. Run-flat tyres means you’ll never be left stranded in the middle of nowhere, and can continue exploring the wilderness. An intelligent driving assistant will also ensure that any new city break is relaxing and safe.
