The famous honey-hue stone that adorns the quaint villages of the Cotswolds are only matched in beauty by the rolling hills and lush green valleys that frame them. From picturesque walks, rustic pubs and romantic castles, the Cotswolds are a charmed country destination.

roadtrip-title: Bourton-on-the-Water to Chipping Campden roadtrip-content: >

Any drive through the Cotswolds is guaranteed to offer scenic countryside and colourful villages that beg to be explored. Start your drive at Bourton-on-the-Water, and head to Lower and Upper Slaughter. Breeze through these quaint villages in the direction of Stow-on-the-Wold, an old market town that typifies the iconic golden shimmer of Cotswolds’ stone. Head toward Moreton-in-Marsh, another quaint market town, and on to Broadway for its famous tower. After taking in the views, finish your drive in Chipping Campden, described as the most perfect high street in England.
